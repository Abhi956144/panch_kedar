1. Kedarnath :

This is the first temple of the Panch Kedars, where the back side of Lord Shiva is worshipped.

2. Tungnath :

Claimed to be the highest temple among the Panch Kedars, where the torso of Shiva is worshipped.

3. Rudranath

The face of Lord Shiva is worshipped here and it is considered an important place for the offering of water to ancestors.

4. Kalpeshwar :

It is the lowest temple of the Panch Kedars, where the Jata (long hair) of Lord Shiva is worshipped, and is situated in a cave.

5. Madhyamaheshwar :

The middle part (navel) of Lord Shiva is worshipped here, which is a lush green and beautiful place.
